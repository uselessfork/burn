DiceFun Documentation

What is DiceFun?

DiceFun is a decentralized PvP dice game built on Solana where players compete against each other for fees. The highest roll wins the pot.

How to Play

  • Connect your Solana wallet using the "Connect Wallet" button
  • Deposit funds into your account
  • Create a new game by setting your bet amount
  • Wait for another player to join, or join an existing open battle
  • Both players roll dice simultaneously
  • Highest roll wins 90% of the pot
  • In case of a tie, both players get their original bet back

Fee Structure

Each game has a 10% platform fee that is split between:

  • Memecoin Holders: A portion goes to holders of our memecoin
  • Platform: Used for maintenance, development, and liquidity

Memecoin Benefits

Holders of the DiceFun memecoin receive a share of platform fees from every game played. The more memecoin you hold, the larger your share of the fees.

Wallet Support

DiceFun supports all major Solana wallets including:

  • Phantom
  • Solflare
  • Backpack
  • Any Solana-compatible wallet

Fair Play

All dice rolls are generated using verifiable randomness on-chain to ensure complete fairness and transparency.

Security

Your wallet remains in your control at all times. DiceFun never has access to your private keys.
